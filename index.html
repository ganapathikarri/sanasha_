Problem Statement

Smallholder farmers struggle to detect early pest infestations due to the high cost and complexity of existing smart traps that depend on cameras or internet connectivity. This leads to delayed action, heavy crop losses, and higher production costs. There is a need for a low-cost, solar-powered, offline pest alert system that can provide timely notifications and support sustainable pest management.

Our Solution

A low-cost, solar-powered pest alert device that uses vibration sensors and pheromone traps to detect pest activity and provide offline LED/sound alerts. It helps farmers identify pests early, take timely action, and reduce crop and pesticide losses—without internet or expensive smart traps.

Benefits

  • Detects pests early to prevent major crop damage.
  • Cuts costs with an affordable, camera-free design.
  • Works reliably offline with simple LED/sound alerts.
  • Reduces pesticide usage and saves farmer expenses.
  • Boosts crop protection without complex technology.

Prototype & How It Works

The prototype combines a bottle-based pheromone trap with a vibration sensor module. A microcontroller monitors vibrations triggered by pest activity and activates an LED/buzzer for instant alerts. A solar panel and rechargeable battery allow continuous operation in the field without internet or maintenance.

Key components

  • Pheromone lure + bottle trap
  • Vibration / piezo sensor
  • Low-power microcontroller and buzzer / LED indicators
  • Small solar panel + rechargeable battery
